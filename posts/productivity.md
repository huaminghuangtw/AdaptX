The goal is _sustainable_ productivity.

把「時鐘」從工作室中移除

	> “Don’t watch the clock; do what it does. Keep going.” — Sam Levenson

	* _Focus on your work, not time!_
	* 重點是「專注（心流）」，而不是「進度」。
